Jurassic Park

Roller Coaster

Embark on a journey to the past, as you bear witness to the most fascinating animals that walked on this planet. Travel through their prehistoric habitats as you fly over these terrifying creatures as they fight for survival. Experience an immersive adventure in a bygone world with one of our most popular rides!

Fantasy Land

Roller Coaster

Experience tranquility as you navigate through a foreign land in our comfortable water ride. Admire the beauty of an Elven kingdom as the river carries you for a relaxing time across magical landscapes. Enjoy the mythical nation during day and night in the longest ride of our amusement park.

Future World

Roller Coaster

Get ready for a high-speed action-packed adventure where the fate of the world lies in your hands! A malfunction has caused all the robots to turn sentient and attack our planet! With sharp turns and massive plunges, help humanity vanquish the machines in the fastest ride of our park.

Wild West

Roller Coaster

Enter Highedge, a town filled with rambunctious cowboys and a thirst for duels. Find and solve the mysteries of a murder as you follow clues left behind by the perpetrator. But watch out, things may not be what they seem in this town. Will you be able to help the local sheriff solve this case?
