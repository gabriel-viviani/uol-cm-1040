Orbitland Awaits!

Travel Through Time and Thrills!

A world of suspense, laughter, knowledge, and entertainment awaits! Join us at Orbitland, where we take you on a ride through eras past and future for maximum awe and enjoyment. Whether to see the dinosaurs in their natural habitat or to be in the thick of a futuristic battle for our planet, Orbitland is sure to bring unforgettable memories to everyone!

Explore Our Zones

Jurassic Park

Soar above the treetops on our DinoFlyer coaster, offering thrilling views and close encounters with these majestic prehistoric beasts.

Fantasy Land

Revel in the illuminated beauty of the Elven kingdom during our enchanted night rides, where the park comes alive with magical light displays.

Future World

Join the resistance and navigate through chaotic cityscapes in our RoboRush roller coaster, where every twist and turn escalates the adrenaline rush.

Wild West

Gallop into the sunset on our Gold Rush Express, where each ride tells a tale of fortune and peril in the rugged frontier.

Satisfy Your Cravings

Garden Grille

Nestled within Fantasy World, Garden Grille serves enchanting garden-to-table dishes sourced from the magical surroundings. Here, guests can relax in a whimsical setting and enjoy fresh flavors that bring fairy tales to life. It's a perfect retreat for families seeking a serene dining experience.

Trattoria Moderna

Ocean's Catch

Set against the rugged backdrop of Wild West, Ocean's Catch brings a taste of the sea to this frontier setting. Guests can indulge in freshly caught ocean-to-table specialties as they soak in the old-western atmosphere. It's the ideal spot for adventurers looking to refuel with hearty, maritime fare.

Fusion Fire

Deep in the heart of Jurassic Park, Fusion Fire ignites palates with a daring mix of Asian and Latin flavors. This eatery offers a thrilling dining adventure where prehistoric ambiance meets contemporary cuisine. It's an explosive culinary experience for bold explorers of the park.
